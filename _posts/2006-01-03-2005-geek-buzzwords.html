  • Firefox
  • Textmate
  • Quicksilver

  • Web 2.0
  • Flickr
  • Del.icio.us
  • Subversion
  • Getting Things Done

It's really unfortunate that I did not keep a record of words earlier in the year. I suspect that this list is a bit biased toward buzzwords I stumbled upon in the second half of the year.
